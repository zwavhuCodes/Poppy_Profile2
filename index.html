About me

Poppy Magome-Maja is a specialist in Information technology. She is respected for her unique perspectives and her balance of contrarian thinking with measured judgment. She is a Social Entrepreneur, Empowerment as well as a Wellness Coach. She is a mother to two boys, Omolemo(6) and Rorisang(9) and has been married to her life partner Tobias Maja for 15 years.

She is passionate about sustainable community development, digital and economic transformation in disadvantaged communities. She advocates for alternative ways and digital platforms to address poverty, unemployment and mental health as these are the top issues threatening the future and stability of South Africa.

It is due to her personal experiences with depression and challenges she has faced in the workplace and society in general that she advocates for mental health as well as women empowerment.

She is extremely passionate about instilling leaders with the necessary skills to build empires and achieve greatness within a highly competitive market.

Mrs South Africa

The Mrs South Africa beauty pageant is a movement that offers married South African women who have real-life careers, families and hobbies an opportunity to compete for this prestigious title.

Glamour and sophistication are part of the criteria, but the women who enter and win have relatable stories that form part of their Mrs South Africa journey. They are smart, ambitious, and care about their family and greater community.
